Why I do it

I have spent the majority of my career in the high tech sector as an engineer enabling the fabrication of semiconductor chips. Now I want to harness the power of those devices and unleash my passion to craft code, bringing ideas to life.

I have always looked for opportunities to integrate my passion for programming into every role I've held. In one instance, an application I conceived and wrote was enthusiastically received by customers and quickly adopted by the business division I worked for to use on a global scale for their technical support teams.

That's what I call job satisfaction. I never want to work another day in my life - and as long as I'm programming, I never will.




"It's only "work" if you'd rather be doing something else" - Dean Kamen
